# CRM Platform Documentation

## Overview
The CRM platform is designed to help businesses manage their relationships and interactions with current and potential customers. It provides a comprehensive solution for tracking customer data, sales pipelines, and communication history.

## Key Features
- **Contact Management:** Organize and manage customer information, including contact details and interaction history.
- **Sales Management:** Monitor and manage your sales pipeline from lead generation to deal closure.
- **Reporting and Analytics:** Generate insights into customer behaviors, sales trends, and overall business performance.
- **Automation:** Automate repetitive tasks such as follow-up emails and reminders.

## Getting Started
1. **Installation**: Follow the installation guide to set up the CRM on your server.
2. **User Setup**: Register your users and assign roles to control access to specific features.
3. **Customization**: Tailor the CRM to fit your business needs by customizing fields and workflows.
